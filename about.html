ABOUT MARIA!

I am a part of Ada Developers Academy Tigers class. Prior to joining Ada, I was a Registered Nurse (RN) but after working through the pandemic I was burnt out and looking for another career path. My brother who is a software developer encouraged me to look into the tech industry and after creating my first landing page I was in love with how much I could create with some basic HTML and CSS. My passion for coding grew when I learned Python and was able to flex my problem solving skills. Despite my brothers support, I never believed I could break into the tech industry AND be successful until I learned about Ada. I was surprised to find a program that not only gives you the technical skills but also has an emphasis on preparing you to be successful in the tech industry while making the industry more diverse and anti-racist. My goal is to land a remote developer job after graduation and one day be experienced enough to volunteer at Ada and give back. I am also passionate about baking, painting, my two fur babies Harper and Scooby, Legos, The Office, and books!
